GOV.UK's Digital Service Manual

Better guidance for teams building digital government services.

The Service Manual is a collection of guidance for anyone involved in building digital services for government. This guidance spans everything from building API’s to service governance. all of the practices involved in building digital

products, from technical specialists to human resourcing experts. The full user demographic therefore consisted of a wide range of different knowledge and digital capabilities. The challenge was building a well organised, easy to understand and navigate body of information that could be understood and relevant to the most, and least, technically apt users across government, helping teams to make better government services.
